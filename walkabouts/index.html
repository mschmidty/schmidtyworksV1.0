The Basics

Your site has 4 pages: Home, Blog, Photography and About. Here's a brief description of each:

Home

The home pages queries your most recent blog posts and most recent photo that you have uploaded. The home page is auto populating.

Blog

The blog page queries the most recent blog posts and puts them in order from the most recently published. Only 10 blog posts appear per page with older posts accessed by clicking older posts (you don't have enough posts yet so this feature does not exist). The excerpt is automatically created by querying the first 55 words of a post.

When you click on "Read More" or on the image, you will be sent to a "single of that post" with older posts at the bottom.

Photography

The Photography page queries all of the photos that you have uploaded. When each picture is selected it takes you to another page where you can cycle through all of you photos.

Note: This is a section that I would greatly like to improve upon. Hopefully there will be a pop up eventually that will let you cycle through the images without leaving the page.

About

A page with information about you on it. Each "About" post you create will add another section to the about page.
