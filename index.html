Wildlife

I'm a Wildlife Technician

By profession, I'm a Wildlife Technician Crew Leader, working on the San Juan National Forest, in Dolores, Colorado. I LOVE MY JOB! I have one of those amazing jobs that allows me to spend about half of my time working outside and half my time at a desk. And yes, as a sudo-supervisor (you notice it's not in my title, hence the sudo) for the government, I fill out a butt load of paperwork. But its worth it, because I get to manage some of the most beautiful lands in the United States.

NEPA

I'm currently learning how to write National Environmental Policy Act (NEPA) documents. I want to document and share everything I learn, hopefully creating a resource that will allow others to critique my work and learn from the mistakes and sucesses I've had. My dream is to turn one day spin this section off into it's own website, providing a resource to those that have to tackle this very strange and complicted process.
